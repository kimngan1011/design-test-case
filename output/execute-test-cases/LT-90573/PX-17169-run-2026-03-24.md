## Observation / Notes

> **Day shift after extension:** The original LS recurred on **Thursdays** (2026/03/19 = Thu). After extending, new lessons recur on **Fridays** starting from 2026/05/08.
>
> **Root cause:** The Extend Recurrence form pre-filled the "Date" field as `2026/05/08` (7 days after the original End Date `2026/05/01`), which is a Friday — not the next Thursday (`2026/05/07`). The weekly recurrence then anchored to Friday.
>
> This day-shift behavior may be a separate bug worth investigating (different from what PX-17169 tests). Skip Closed Dates logic itself works correctly.
